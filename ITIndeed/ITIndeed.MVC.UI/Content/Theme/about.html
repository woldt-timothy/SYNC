    Commence, Connect and Create Business Relationships

    Our aim is to connect students with employers. We recognize through direct personal experience that it can be very difficult during college for students to connect with employers. We want to bridge this separation. Sync provides a service that allows students to communicate with Employers and vise versa. We want employers and students to be able to communicate on a one-to-one experience.

    We also recognize that employers are searching for a particular skill for the future employees they hire. Through our Employer Blog, we wish to help employers articulate what they are looking for in an employee.

    Our Vision

    Sync desires to establish lifelong connections by providing a tool for students and employers to engage in conversation and create relationships.
